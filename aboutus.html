About Us

PAWS Darwin Australia is a not for profit animal welfare ornganisation with charitable status, located in Berrimah dedicated to provide care for unwanted and stray animals. The main goal of this ornagisation is to find a home for these animals to loving families. We also help people find lost pets and educate the community on animal issues and concerns which include proper training, healthcare, desexing pets through school excursions and also advertising campaigns.

All animals from PAWS Darwin are adopted out sterilised (desexed), micro chipped, up to date on vaccinations, or with vouchers for these to be used at one of our participating veterinary clinics within a set time frame post adoption. All of our animals are guaranteed to be up to date with worming, tick, and flea treatment upon adoption. Our dogs also come with four free behavioural class vouchers.

We provide the best service to our pets so that they can be at their best when you take them home. Please consider adopting a PAWS pet when searching for your next best mate!
